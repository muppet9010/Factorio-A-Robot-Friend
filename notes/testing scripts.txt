Make a robot at spawn and make it walk to the set location under a job:

/sc __a_robot_friend__
local targetPosition = {100,100}
local robot = MOD.Interfaces.RobotManager.CreateRobot(game.surfaces[1], {2,2}, 1)
local job = MOD.Interfaces.Jobs.MoveToLocation(1, targetPosition, game.surfaces[1])
MOD.Interfaces.RobotManager.AssignRobotToJob(robot, job)
