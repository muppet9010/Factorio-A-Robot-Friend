Make a robot at spawn:

/c __a_robot_friend__ MOD.Interfaces.RobotManager.CreateRobot(game.surfaces[1], {0,0})





Make the robot walk around:

/c __a_robot_friend__ MOD.Interfaces.Actions.walking(global.RobotManager.robots[1].entity, defines.direction.east)

/c __a_robot_friend__ MOD.Interfaces.Actions.walking(global.RobotManager.robots[1].entity, defines.direction.west)

/c __a_robot_friend__ MOD.Interfaces.Actions.walking(global.RobotManager.robots[1].entity, nil)
